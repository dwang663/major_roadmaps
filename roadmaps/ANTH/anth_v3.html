Requirements

Requirements
B.A. Anthropology
12 course credits (incl senior req)
Concentrations optional
At least 1 intro or intermediate (1000-2000 level) course in each of 3 subfields
At least 3 advanced (3000-4000 level) courses, not incl ANTH 4071, ANTH 4091, or seminar senior essay
2 ANTH electives
Of the 12 course credits, at least 9 must be ANTH courses, including senior requirement
Remaining courses can include 2 or 3 cognate courses in other departments or programs with DUS approval
