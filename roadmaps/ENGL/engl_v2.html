Substitutions

B.A. English: Creative Writing Concentration
DRST 0001 and DRST 0002 or ENGL 1029 and ENGL 1030 may substitute for one foundational course; 2 upper-level courses with overlapping material may substitute for one foundational course with DUS permission;

Yale College First Destinations

First destination refers to the postgraduate plans of Yale College graduates six months after graduation. The pursuits of Yale graduates include employment, graduate school, independent research, military service, and more.
